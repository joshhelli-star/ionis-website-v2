The Reporting Problem

Most B2B businesses are running campaigns across Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn, and email while managing their pipeline in a CRM and none of these platforms talk to each other. The result is that nobody has a clear picture of what is actually working.

Your data lives in ten different places

Google Ads is in one tab. Meta is in another. LinkedIn is somewhere else. Your CRM has the conversion data. Your email platform has the engagement numbers. Nobody sees all of it in one place and nobody has time to pull it all together.

Reporting takes hours every week

Someone on your team is manually exporting data from multiple platforms, pasting it into a spreadsheet, formatting it, and building a summary. That work is repeated every week. It produces a report that is already out of date by the time it is sent.

You cannot see which channel is driving revenue

Knowing that Google Ads generated 50 clicks tells you very little. Knowing that those 50 clicks produced three qualified leads that turned into two clients worth £30,000 tells you where to put next month's budget. Most businesses cannot make that connection.

Generic dashboard tools give you generic results

Looker Studio and Power BI are powerful, but they are built for anyone. That means the dashboard ends up looking like every other dashboard, showing metrics that are close to what you need but not quite right, and requiring constant manual adjustment.

How It Works

01

Data and Requirements Audit

We map every platform you use, identify what data exists and where it lives, and work with you to define exactly what the dashboard needs to show and who needs to see it. We also identify any tracking gaps that would create blind spots.

02

Data Pipeline Build

We build the automated pipelines that pull data from each source on a schedule and push it into a central data layer. This runs automatically in the background so your dashboard always has current data without anyone touching it.

03

Bespoke Dashboard Design and Build

We design and build the dashboard to your exact specifications. Branded to your business, structured around your metrics, and built with your team's workflow in mind. Not a template with your logo on it.

04

User Setup and Handover

We set up user accounts and access controls, walk your team through how to use the dashboard, and document everything. You own what we build and you are not dependent on us to keep it running.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from just using Looker Studio?

Looker Studio is a tool that anyone can use to build a dashboard. What we build is a bespoke system designed specifically for your business, your data, and your team. The difference is like the difference between buying a template website and having one built for you. The end result looks different, works differently, and does not require you to compromise on what you actually need.

Can clients and team members have their own logins?

Yes. We build user authentication and role-based access into dashboards where this is needed. Each user sees exactly what they should see and nothing they should not. This is particularly useful for agencies with multiple clients or businesses with multiple departments.

How long does it take to build?

A focused single-use-case dashboard can be live within one to two weeks. Multi-source builds with attribution modelling, custom logins, and multiple user roles take three to four weeks. We will give you a specific timeline after reviewing your requirements.

Will the data update automatically?

Yes. Once built, the data pipelines refresh on a schedule, usually daily or more frequently depending on the source. Nobody needs to touch anything to keep the numbers current.

What if our tracking is not set up correctly?

We audit this at the start. If there are gaps in your tracking, we flag them and in most cases can fix them as part of the build. A dashboard is only as good as the data going into it, so we take this seriously before building anything.

Do we own the dashboard when it is built?

Yes. Everything we build is yours. We document how it works and hand it over so you are not dependent on us to keep it running. We are available for ongoing support and expansion if you want it, but it is not a requirement.
