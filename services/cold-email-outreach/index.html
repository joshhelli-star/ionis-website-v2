Stop doing outreach by hand. We build automated systems that prospect, personalise, and follow up so your team spends time on conversations, not admin.

Why Outbound Breaks Down

Most outbound fails for the same reasons. Lists go stale. Follow-ups get forgotten. Emails are generic so they get ignored. Reps spend hours on research and barely any time actually selling. The problem is not effort. It is the absence of a system.

Your lists go stale faster than your team can work them

Prospects change jobs, companies pivot, contacts go cold. Without automated enrichment you are working from data that was accurate six months ago.

Follow-up falls through the cracks

One email is not a campaign. Most replies come from the third or fourth touch, but manual follow-up is inconsistent. Reps forget, get busy, or give up too early.

Generic emails get ignored

Buyers receive dozens of cold emails a day. Anything that reads like a template goes straight to the bin. Personalisation at scale is impossible to do manually.

Your reps are researchers, not sellers

If your salespeople are spending hours building lists and writing individual emails, they are not selling. That time has a real cost.

Nothing is tracked properly

Without a system logging every send, reply, and follow-up in your CRM, you have no idea what is working or what needs following up.

What a Cold Email Outreach System Does

We build end-to-end outreach systems that handle the repetitive work automatically, so the only thing left for your team to do is respond to interested prospects.

→
Automated Prospect Research

The system sources and enriches leads based on your ideal client profile. Job title, company size, industry, tech stack, recent activity. You define who you want to reach. The system finds them and keeps the data clean.

→
Personalised Email Copy at Scale

Each email is written using data about the prospect and their business. Not a name swap in a template. Actual relevant personalisation that reads like it was written for them, generated automatically.

→
Multi-Channel Sequences

Email sequences run automatically with the right timing and spacing. LinkedIn touchpoints can be layered in alongside email. Follow-ups trigger based on behaviour, not a calendar reminder someone has to remember.

→
Deliverability Infrastructure

Sending cold email without the right infrastructure gets you marked as spam. We set up and manage domain warming, sending limits, SPF, DKIM, and DMARC so your emails land in inboxes.

→
CRM Sync

Every send, open, reply, and bounce is logged automatically. Your CRM stays current without anyone touching it manually. Replies that need attention get flagged. See how we handle this in our CRM Automation service.

→
Offer Strategy & Lead Magnet Development

Before a single email goes out, we work with you to sharpen your offer. Most cold email fails not because of the system but because the positioning is weak. We help you identify the strongest angle for your market, develop lead magnets that generate responses, and build in guarantees that remove the risk for your prospects. This is the part most agencies skip — and why their campaigns underperform.

→
Direct Slack Access

You get direct access to us via a shared Slack channel throughout the engagement. Fast answers, quick iterations, no waiting on email threads.

Example Workflows

01

New Lead List to Booked Meeting

A list of target companies is added to the system. The system enriches each contact, finds the right decision maker, writes a personalised opening email and follow-up sequence, sends across email and LinkedIn, logs all activity to the CRM, and flags replies for the sales rep to pick up. The rep only gets involved when someone responds.

02

Dormant CRM Contacts

Contacts in your CRM that have gone quiet over the past 60 to 90 days get automatically identified. The system writes a re-engagement sequence with a fresh angle, sends it, and routes any positive responses back to the relevant rep.

03

Trigger-Based Outreach

A prospect visits your pricing page, downloads a piece of content, or hits another intent signal. The system detects the trigger and automatically starts a relevant sequence within minutes, while the interest is still fresh.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will this damage my email deliverability?

Not if it is set up correctly. Deliverability problems come from poor infrastructure, bad list quality, and sending too much too fast. We handle domain warming, sending limits, and technical setup as part of every build. Done properly, a cold email system protects your deliverability rather than harming it.

Will the emails sound like they were written by AI?

No. The personalisation uses real data about the prospect and their business. The copy is written to sound like it came from a person, because the output is reviewed and the prompts are built to produce natural, relevant emails. If something reads like a robot wrote it, it does not go out.

Can we approve emails before they send?

Yes. We can build approval steps into the workflow so nothing sends without a human sign-off. For most clients this is not necessary once they trust the output, but it is always an option.

Is cold email legal? What about GDPR?

Cold email to business contacts is legal under GDPR when done correctly. B2B cold email is treated differently from consumer marketing. We build sequences that comply with UK and US regulations. If you have specific legal concerns, we recommend getting advice from your own legal team, but for most B2B use cases this is straightforward.

How long does it take to set up?

A basic outreach system can be live within one to two weeks. More complex builds with multiple segments, CRM integrations, and trigger-based workflows take longer. We will give you a clear timeline after the discovery call.

What results can I expect?

We will not give you made-up numbers. Results depend on your market, your offer, and your list quality. What we can tell you is that a well-built system will outperform manual outreach on consistency and volume every time. We will set realistic expectations during the discovery call.
