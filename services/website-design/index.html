A Website That Nobody Finds Is Not Doing Its Job

Most B2B websites are built to look good. The design is clean, the branding is consistent, and the messaging sounds right. Then it goes live and nothing happens.

The problem is almost always the same. The site was built without a real strategy for how people would actually find it. No keyword research. No structure built around how buyers search. No content that gives Google a reason to rank it above anyone else.

We build websites differently. SEO strategy comes first. The structure, the pages, the headings, and the content are all built around the specific searches your ideal clients make when they are ready to buy. The design comes after — and it is built to convert the traffic that strategy brings in.

How We Work

Every build follows the same discipline: strategy before design, design before development, content and structure agreed before anything is built.

01

Audit and Strategy

We start with an SEO and competitor audit — even if you already have a site. We identify what keywords your ideal clients are searching for, who is currently ranking for them, and what gaps we can exploit. From there we agree on the site structure: how many pages, what each page targets, and how they link together. You see and approve this before any design or content work begins.

02

Design

We work to your brand if you have one, or build a visual identity as part of the project if you do not. Designs are produced in Figma and reviewed before anything goes into development. You approve what it will look like before we write a line of code.

03

Build, Launch, and Handover

Content is drafted, reviewed, and refined. Pages are built and tested. Once everything is approved, we launch. We then provide a handover document covering how the site is structured, what each page is targeting, and what to do next to continue building visibility over time.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a full website build take?

For a standard B2B service site — homepage, service pages, about, and contact — typically four to eight weeks from strategy sign-off to launch. Larger sites or projects requiring significant content research take longer. We agree a timeline before starting.

Do I need to provide all the content?

No. We handle research, drafting, and refinement of all page content as part of the build. We will need your input to understand your services, your clients, and your positioning — but you do not need to write anything yourself.

What if I already have a brand and design guidelines?

We work within your existing brand. If you have fonts, colours, and a logo, we build to those. If your brand needs refreshing as part of the project, we can advise on that too.

How is this different from just hiring a web designer?

Most web designers build what you ask for. We start with SEO strategy and build the site around it. The structure, content, and copy are all built to rank for the searches your ideal clients are making. A site that looks good but cannot be found by the right people is not delivering value.

Will the site rank straight away after launch?

No. Organic visibility builds over time as Google indexes and assesses the site. What we do is give you the strongest possible foundation from day one — the right structure, the right content, and the right technical setup — so that rankings build faster than they would on a poorly optimised site.

Can you also help with ongoing SEO after the site is live?

We can advise on ongoing strategy. For active ongoing work — new pages, content updates, link building — we can scope that as a separate project or point you toward what to prioritise yourself using the handover documentation we provide.
