We are a UK-based AI automation agency that builds systems for B2B businesses. No fluff, no inflated promises — just practical automation that saves time and drives measurable results.

Who We Are

Ionis AI was founded by Josh, an automation specialist who spent years watching B2B businesses waste time on manual processes that had no business being done by hand.

The pattern was always the same. Smart teams, good products, real ambition — held back by repetitive admin, disconnected tools, and workflows that depended entirely on people remembering to do things. Cold outreach done manually. Invoices sent late. CRM data nobody trusted. Reports that took hours to build every week.

The solution was not more software. Every business already has enough software. The solution was connecting what they already had, automating what follows a predictable pattern, and building systems that run in the background while the team focuses on work that actually requires them.

That is what Ionis AI does.

How We Work

We are not a large agency with account managers and project coordinators between you and the person doing the work. When you work with Ionis AI, you work directly with Josh. Faster decisions, clearer communication, no briefing chain.

We start with the problem

Every engagement starts with a discovery call where we understand what you actually do, where the friction is, and what a successful outcome looks like. We do not pitch a service before we understand whether it is the right fit.

We build to your stack, not ours

We use Make, n8n, Zapier, direct API integrations, and custom code depending on what the problem requires. We work with the tools you already use rather than asking you to migrate to something new.

You own what we build

No proprietary systems. No ongoing licence fees to access your own automation. Everything is documented and handed over so your team understands how it works and you are never dependent on us to keep it running.

We tell you honestly if it is not worth building

Not every idea is a good candidate for automation. If the discovery call reveals that the build cost would not justify the time saved, we will tell you. We would rather lose a project than deliver something that does not deliver value.

Our Approach to AI

We use AI where it adds genuine value, not because it is impressive.

Where we use it

AI is particularly useful for tasks that involve processing natural language, extracting structured data from unstructured inputs, generating personalised content at scale, and making decisions based on patterns in data.

Where we do not

We do not automate decisions that require genuine human judgement. We do not remove humans from processes where their involvement matters. The goal is to make the people using these systems more effective, not to replace them entirely.

The result

Systems that run in the background, handle the predictable, and surface what needs a person. Your team spends less time on work that follows a pattern and more time on work that actually requires them.

Where We Work

Ionis AI is based in the UK. We work with B2B businesses across the UK and US, and take on projects from other markets where the work is a good fit.

Most of our clients are service businesses — agencies, consultancies, professional services firms — though we work across a range of industries. If you sell to other businesses and your team is spending time on things that should be automated, we are likely the right fit regardless of your sector.
